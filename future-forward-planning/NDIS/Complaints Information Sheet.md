# Complaints Information Sheet
**SC Partnering Pty Ltd (trading as Future Forward Planning)**
*Plan Management Services*

---

## We want to hear from you

If you are unhappy with our service, or if something has gone wrong, please tell us. We take all complaints seriously and will treat you with respect and fairness.

Making a complaint will not affect your services in any way.

---

## What happens after you complain?

| Step | What we do | When |
|------|-----------|------|
| **1. We acknowledge your complaint** | We contact you to confirm we have received it | Within 2 business days |
| **2. We assess your complaint** | We work out what the complaint is about and how urgent it is | Within 3 business days |
| **3. We investigate** | We look into what happened | Within 10 business days |
| **4. We resolve and tell you the outcome** | We let you know what we found and what we will do | Within 14 business days |

We will keep you updated by email at each step.

If your complaint is complex it may take a little longer. We will always let you know if this is the case.

---

## If you are not satisfied

If we cannot resolve your complaint, or if you are not happy with our response, you can contact the **NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission**:

- **Phone:** 1800 035 544
- **Website:** www.ndiscommission.gov.au
- **Online complaint form:** www.ndiscommission.gov.au/about/complaints

You can also get free advocacy support from the **National Disability Advocacy Program (NDAP):**
- **Phone:** 1800 643 787

---

## You have rights

- You can make a complaint at any time.
- You can have a support person or advocate help you.
- Your complaint will be kept confidential.
- Making a complaint will not affect your NDIS plan or your services.
- You can ask us to communicate with you in a different way if needed.
