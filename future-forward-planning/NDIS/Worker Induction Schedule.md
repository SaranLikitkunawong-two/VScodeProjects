# Worker Induction Schedule
**SC Partnering Pty Ltd (trading as Future Forward Planning)**
*PM-POL-004 — Human Resources and Worker Screening Policy*

> **Run-sheet for the worker's first day.** The induction coordinator (Director) works through each block with the new worker. Total time: approximately 2 hours. The Worker Induction Checklist (sign-off form) is completed at the end.

---

## Before the Day — Pre-Commencement (Coordinator)

These must be done before the worker arrives. They are not part of the 2-hour clock.

| Item | Owner |
|------|-------|
| NDIS Worker Screening Clearance confirmed and logged | Director |
| Employment contract or contractor agreement signed | Director |
| Portal login and email account created | Director |
| MFA enabled on all accounts | Director |
| Induction Checklist printed or open on screen | Director |

---

## Induction Day Run-Sheet

### Block 1 — Welcome and Context (10 minutes)

**Who:** Director with new worker, in person or video call.

- Welcome and introductions
- Overview of Future Forward Planning: what we do, how plan management works, who our participants are
- Explain the structure of today's induction and why each part matters
- Confirm the worker has their NDIS Worker Screening Clearance in hand

---

### Block 2 — NDIS Commission Online Training (90 minutes)

**Who:** Worker completes independently on their device. Coordinator is available for questions.

The worker completes the following official NDIS Commission module:

#### Mandatory: *Quality, Safety and You* — Worker Orientation Module
- **URL:** [training.ndiscommission.gov.au](https://training.ndiscommission.gov.au)
- **Duration:** ~90 minutes
- **Format:** Self-paced online e-learning with short video segments and knowledge checks
- **Certificate:** Non-expiring, transferable — worker saves the PDF and emails a copy to the Director

**What it covers:**
- The NDIS, its purpose, and the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission
- Rights of people with disability
- The NDIS Code of Conduct and worker obligations
- Identifying and responding to risk
- Abuse, neglect, and exploitation — recognition and reporting
- Supporting participants to achieve their goals

> If the worker has completed this module at a previous NDIS provider, they do not need to repeat it — they provide their existing certificate instead. Proceed directly to Block 3.

---

### Block 3 — Organisation Policies: Key Highlights (20 minutes)

**Who:** Director walks the worker through the highlights. The worker is not expected to read all 17 policies in full today — they confirm they have received them and will read each one within 5 business days of commencing.

The Director verbally covers:

| Policy | Key Points to Highlight |
|--------|------------------------|
| PM-POL-005 Privacy and Confidentiality | Participant data is confidential; no sharing outside authorised channels; storage in the portal only |
| PM-POL-001 Incident Management | What counts as an incident; how to report; NDIS Commission notification timeframes |
| PM-POL-006 Code of Conduct | Professional boundaries; zero tolerance for abuse or exploitation; social media boundaries |
| PM-POL-011 Fraud Prevention | How plan management fraud occurs; red flags to watch for; reporting obligations |
| PM-POL-015 Cyber Security | Password manager, MFA, phishing, device use, no personal email for participant data |

The worker signs the Policy Suite acknowledgement on the Induction Checklist (Part C) confirming they have received the full policy suite and will complete their review within 5 business days.

---

### Block 4 — Systems Walkthrough and Sign-Off (20 minutes)

**Who:** Director demonstrates; worker follows along on their own device.

| System | What to Cover |
|--------|---------------|
| Future Forward Planning portal | Login, participant record structure, invoice queue, registers (Incident, Complaints, Risk) |
| NDIA PACE portal | Login confirmation, how to check plan budgets and claim status (if applicable to role) |
| Email and calendar | Client communication standards, response time expectations |
| Password manager | How to use it; policy: no passwords written down or saved in browser |

To close out, the worker and Director sign the **Worker Induction Checklist** (Parts A–F), the certificate from Block 2 is confirmed received and filed, and the Conflict of Interest declaration (PM-POL-012) is signed.
