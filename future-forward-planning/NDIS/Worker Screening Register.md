# Worker Screening Register
**SC Partnering Pty Ltd (trading as Future Forward Planning)**
*PM-POL-004 — Human Resources and Worker Screening Policy*

> All workers who will have more than incidental contact with NDIS participants must hold a current NDIS Worker Screening Clearance before commencing in that role. This register must be updated whenever a clearance is obtained, renewed, or a new worker is engaged. Monitored by the Director. Calendar reminders are set 60 days prior to each expiry date to initiate renewal.

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Application submitted or awaiting submission; worker has not yet received clearance |
| Cleared | Valid NDIS Worker Screening Clearance held; number and expiry confirmed |
| Expired | Clearance has lapsed; renewal required before worker continues in role |
| Refused | Application refused; worker must not be engaged in a role involving participant contact |
| Excluded | Worker is subject to an exclusion; must not be engaged |

---

## Renewal Monitoring

Calendar reminders are set 60 days prior to each clearance expiry date. The Director is responsible for initiating renewal before the clearance lapses. No worker may continue in a role involving participant contact with an expired clearance.
