# Incident Register
**SC Partnering Pty Ltd (trading as Future Forward Planning)**
*PM-POL-001 — Incident Management Policy*

> This register must be updated whenever an incident is identified and at each stage of the response process. Reviewed monthly by the Director. No incident may be closed without documented corrective actions. Retain all records for a minimum of 7 years.

---

## How to Use This Register

- Assign a unique reference number in the format: **INC-[YEAR]-[NUMBER]** (e.g. INC-2026-001)
- Open a new row when an incident is identified — cross-reference the Incident Report Form (Attachment A), which serves as the complete investigation record including findings, root cause, corrective actions, and post-incident review. No separate investigation report is required.
- Update the row at each stage of the response
- An incident may only be marked **Closed** when all corrective actions are completed and documented
- All open incidents must be reviewed at the monthly Director review

## Severity Rating Reference

**Risk Rating = Likelihood (1–5) × Consequence (1–5)**

| Rating | Score | Required Action |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| Low | 1–4 | Accept and monitor; corrective action at next review |
| Medium | 5–9 | Treat with controls; corrective action within 10 business days |
| High | 10–19 | Immediate action required; Post-Incident Review within 20 business days |
| Extreme | 20–25 | Escalate immediately; halt operations if necessary; Post-Incident Review within 20 business days |
