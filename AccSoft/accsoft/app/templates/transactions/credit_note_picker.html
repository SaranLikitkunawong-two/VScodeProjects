What kind of credit note are you creating?

Customer

Customer Credit Note

Credit issued to a customer — reduces the amount they owe you. Use when a customer was over-billed, returned goods, or is owed a refund.

Supplier

Supplier Credit Note

Credit received from a supplier — reduces the amount you owe them. Use when a supplier over-charged, you returned goods, or they owe you a refund.

Tip: credit notes can also be created directly from an existing invoice or bill — just open it and click "Create Credit Note".
